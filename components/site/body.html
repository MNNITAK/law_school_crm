About the College

A house of advocacy in the heart of Jankipuram Vistar — part of the respected City Group of Colleges.

City Law College is affiliated to the University of Lucknow (College Code 1238) and offers the 5-year integrated BA LL.B (Hons.) and the 3-year LL.B. On the AKTU–CDRI Road in Sector 9, the campus pairs serious academics with a culture of practice — moot courts, a legal-aid clinic and structured internships across Lucknow's chambers and courts.

Our Programmes

Two paths into the profession of law

Both programmes are merit-based and governed by the ordinances of the University of Lucknow.

5-Year Integrated · After Class 12

BA LL.B (Hons.)

Enter law directly after Class 12 with a humanities foundation built in.

  • Eligibility: 10+2 with min. 45% (40% SC/ST)
  • Intake: 120 seats · Merit-based
  • Includes: Arts core + full law curriculum + internships
3-Year · Graduate Entry

LL.B

For graduates of any discipline ready to commit fully to the law.

  • Eligibility: Graduation with min. 50%
  • Intake: 120 seats · Merit-based
  • Includes: Moot court, legal-aid clinic, bar-exam prep
